    Count the number of stars in the sky

  • K-means, Outliear in sub-region, DFS, opencv

    • At one night, I took some photos for the stars at LaJolla shore. I was curious about how many stars are in my photo. So, I tried to implement a K-means algorithm to classify the stars in the sky and other dark area. The result is not very ideal. Then, I proposed a solution that cuts the image into several sub-regions and find outlier pixels in these sub-regions. It is not only effective but also efficient. In this way, I was able to identify the stars in the sky. Finally, I used a DFS algorithm to count the number of star in the sky.
